What Is Kolenu?

Kolenu is a community-driven prayer learning app.

  • Supports Bar/Bat Mitzvah preparation
  • Preserves traditional nusach recordings
  • Makes prayer learning accessible worldwide

Only traditional liturgical recordings — no modern or copyrighted music.

Learners need to hear what they will encounter in synagogue: the melodies your cantor chants, the trop your tutor teaches.

Kolenu is fully open-source and community-supported.
The complete app code is publicly available on GitHub — nothing hidden.

We Need Your Voice 🎙

Volunteer Recording Project

Kolenu is building a growing library of traditional nusach recordings.

Your recording helps:

  • Expand the nusach library for learners
  • Preserve authentic traditions
  • Give credit to contributors

We invite volunteers to contribute:

  • Record traditional nusach (Ashkenazi, Sephardi, and other authentic traditions)
  • No professional studio required — a phone recording at home is welcome
  • Short segments are valuable
  • Contributors are credited in the app

This is not a commercial music project.
This is community preservation.

Especially valuable recordings include:

  • Weekday Shacharit nusach
  • Shabbat Torah trop
  • Haftorah trop
  • Core prayers for Bar Mitzvah students
