What Is Kolenu?

Kolenu is a community-driven prayer learning app dedicated to preserving and teaching traditional nusach.

It is designed to:

  • Help teens prepare confidently for Bar/Bat Mitzvah
  • Support adults reconnecting with synagogue life
  • Provide authentic traditional nusach recordings
  • Make Jewish prayer accessible worldwide

No modern compositions.
No commercial music.
Only traditional nusach — preserved and shared by the community.

Because learners need to hear what they will actually encounter in synagogue: the melodies your cantor chants, the trop your tutor teaches, the sounds that connect you to generations before you.

Traditional nusach is not just music — it is continuity.

Kolenu is an open, community-supported project.
View the source code on GitHub.

We Need Your Voice 🎙

Volunteer Recording Project

Kolenu is building a growing library of traditional nusach recordings.

We invite volunteers to contribute:

  • Record traditional nusach (Ashkenazi, Sephardi, and other authentic traditions)
  • No professional studio required — a phone recording at home is welcome
  • Short segments are valuable
  • Contributors are credited in the app

This is not a commercial music project.
This is community preservation.

Especially valuable recordings include:

  • Weekday Shacharit nusach
  • Shabbat Torah trop
  • Haftorah trop
  • Core prayers for Bar Mitzvah students
Submit a Recording

Kolenu accepts only traditional nusach and public-domain liturgical melodies.
Please do not submit copyrighted compositions.
