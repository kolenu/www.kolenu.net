🎙 Contribute Your Voice to Kolenu

Our Shared Tradition

Kolenu is a community-supported project helping Jews of all backgrounds learn traditional Hebrew prayer with confidence.

When you contribute a recording, you are strengthening our shared tradition and helping others pray with clarity and understanding.

Every voice matters.

What We're Looking For

We welcome:

  • Traditional nusach (public-domain melodies)
  • Clear, steady recordings of complete prayers
  • Submissions from adults
  • Submissions from teens (with parent/guardian consent)

Recordings should:

  • Be your own voice
  • Follow a traditional melody, or one you have permission to share
  • Be clear and free from heavy background noise

How Your Recording Is Used

Your submission may be:

  • Included inside the Kolenu app
  • Used as a learning example for prayer practice
  • Shared in related Kolenu educational materials

Kolenu is operated by DigiMint Inc., and recordings are used for educational purposes only.

We may review submissions before publishing. We may also remove content at our discretion, and we aim to honor reasonable removal requests.

Attribution & Privacy

We honor every contributor while respecting your privacy and comfort level.

You choose how you'd like to be credited.

👦 Teen Contributors (Under 18)

To protect minors, we never publish last names.

Your child may be credited as:

  • First Name + City
    Example: Daniel (Age 13), Toronto

Parents/guardians must approve all teen submissions before publication.

👨 Adult Contributors (18+)

Adults may choose fuller attribution if desired.

You may be credited as:

  • Full Name + Description
    Example: Rachel Cohen, Cantor & Educator, Vancouver, Canada

Thank You

Kolenu exists because of community generosity.

By sharing your voice, you help others learn to pray with confidence.

Together, we strengthen our shared tradition.
